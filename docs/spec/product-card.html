Product Card.

Flagship scan surface. 1:1 square image, StatusBadge overlay top-left, save heart top-right, content block below. Hover adds a 2.5px lime accent shadow + amethyst border — no translate. Click navigates to the PDP at /product/:id.

Not this

✗ Don't · open a modal on click

Clicking the card navigates to /product/:id. No modal overlay. Back-button, deep-link, share — all must work. (PC-11)

Modal overlay for product detail was the v3 pattern; it's deprecated.

✗ Don't · hide the heart behind hover

The save heart is always visible. No opacity-0 group-hover:opacity-100. (PC-06)

Hover-reveal fails on touch devices and breaks discoverability.

✗ Don't · add per-card action icons

No add-to-list "+" button, no share icon, no compare toggle. Card surface stays minimal. (PC-08)

Multi-step actions live on the PDP. Card is a scan surface, not an action panel.
