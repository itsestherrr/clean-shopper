Foundations · Design System

design system.

Editorial, considered, trustworthy. Amethyst ink carries identity and the signature flat-shadow. Lime is the accent — hero moments, premium states. Slate is the interactive accent — links, focus rings, info banners. Lilac holds the favorite heart. Stone groups categories. Sage/amber/scarlet exist only to carry product safety status — never decorative.

Amethyst ramp

ink hierarchy · 5 stops

Amethyst is the only color that needs a full ramp because it carries all text hierarchy — primary, secondary, tertiary, placeholder, and muted surface. Other brand colors keep one tint because they only do one job.

Role lock
Amethyst is the signature. Ink, borders, and the 4px flat card shadow. Never implies safety.
Lime is the accent. One or two per screen — hero moments, premium badges. Never the default card shadow.
Slate is interactive. Links, focus rings, info banners. Never a card shadow.
Stone carries all categories, one color. Lilac holds the favorite heart, tone-on-tone.

10 · Category tags

one color · no judgment

All product categories share one token: stone-tint. Categories are organizational tags — they should never imply "safe" or "dangerous." Differentiation is the label text, not the hue.
