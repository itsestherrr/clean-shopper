Button System: Three Tiers

Primary (solid fill), secondary (outline), ghost (text only). Each level is visually tied to the primary color but at decreasing emphasis.

Primary Solid fill. One per view. The single most important action.
Secondary Outline. Supporting actions. Clearly related to primary but lower emphasis.
Ghost Text only. Tertiary actions. Cancel, dismiss, "see more."
